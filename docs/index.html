GEOG0125: Advanced Topics in Social Geographic Data Science

Welcome


Welcome to GEOG0125: Advanced Topics in Social Geographic Data Science, one of the optional modules for this MSc programme (Social and Geographic Data Science). This module has been designed as an advanced topics module to learn data science concepts and methods, and to apply them in the domains of social science and geography. The module will introduce concepts such as statistical inference and machine learning methodologies.

In the first three weeks, you will be introduced to descriptive and inferential statistical techniques in RStudio. Afterwards, in week 4, 5 and 6, we will cover a variety of unique machine learning techniques which includes Deep Learning, Convolutional Neural Networks and GeoAI in Python. In week 7, 8, 9 and 10, we will introduced to Bayesian statistics using INLA to get perform geospatial analysis of point, areal and raster data. This will cover Spatial Temporal Modelling as well as SPDE-INLA. Lastly, you will gain the practical skills for building dashboards for Web & Geo-visualisation using R-Shiny.
